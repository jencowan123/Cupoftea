About Cup of Tea

Cup of Tea is a well-being app supporting the emotional needs of new parents. Cup of Tea addresses the physical/mental health gap with self assessment tools, messaging support and links to professional services and positive reinforcement. With Cup of Tea, you can:

Identify and address peri natal depression in yourself and others.

Access messaging support on learn how best to communicate concerns about your own mental health and that of others with clarity and without judgement.

Identify and link to professional, community and/or social support networks.

“The average British woman has her first baby at thirty: she will have been using the internet since her early teens, and has perhaps been on Facebook for ten years and Twitter or LinkedIn for five. She and her partner, if she has one, probably updated her friends and family about her pregnancy through WhatsApp and Skype. And yet if she finds her mood dipping in the first weeks of parenthood, she’ll be expected to call her GP to make an appointment, travel to the surgery with her baby, and – if she one of the lucky ones who are offered professional mental health services – potentially arrange childcare so she can get to further appointments, which may be many miles from home. Internet and smartphone technology could make this much easier: the challenge is to put it into practice.” -Clare Harding of The Big White Wall
